# Membo

One place to learn, manage money, and plan your time — with an AI assistant that actually remembers what you're working on.

Most AI tools forget everything the moment you close the tab. Membo keeps a persistent memory per topic, so your AI conversations build on each other over time rather than starting from scratch every session.

## What it does

### Learn
Create notes for anything you want to study — a concept, a language, a trip you're planning. Each note has its own AI chat that knows your content and updates it as you go. Import a PDF and the AI absorbs it into your note. Import multiple PDFs and it merges them into one clean, structured knowledge base without dropping a single detail.

When you're ready to test yourself, flip into flashcard mode. Cards are scheduled using the SM-2 spaced repetition algorithm — the same method behind Anki — so you review things right before you forget them, not on a fixed loop. Rate each card (again / hard / good / easy), build a daily streak, and track your lifetime accuracy.

Write an essay and get it graded against a custom rubric. The built-in IELTS rubric scores across all four official criteria (Task Achievement, Coherence, Lexical Resource, Grammar) with half-band precision — useful for serious test prep, or as a template for building your own.

When a note is ready to share, make it public and send the link. No account needed to view it.

### Finance
Track all your money in one place: cash, bank accounts, savings, credit cards, investments, and debt. Log transactions, set up recurring installments, and plan monthly budgets. The AI assistant lets you manage everything conversationally — ask what you spent last month, log a new expense, or check your budget — instead of clicking through forms.

### Calendar
Manage events, tasks, and study sessions with a chat assistant that understands natural language. Connect your Google Calendar so everything stays in sync. When you need focused study time, the AI finds open slots around your existing schedule and books them for you.
